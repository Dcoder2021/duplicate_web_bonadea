B E S P O K E  &  E L E G A N T

Destination Wedding in the magnificent Hemel-en-Aarde Valley, Hermanus in South Africa.

Unforgettable Wedding Venue with endless views across heavenly vineyards…

Dea Private Estate is 2.5km from Hermanus, the gem of the Overberg region and in the heart of the Cape Whale Coast, Western Cape, South Africa. We are a stone’s throw away from Cape Town International Airport & which is 110km away.

Your special occasion will be framed by spectacular scenery, an array of exquisite settings, and breathtaking locations that create the ideal backdrop for a truly unforgettable celebration.

All packages include exclusive use of the entire venue complex for your bespoke wedding – a bespoke wedding venue in Hermanus!

It boasts endless views of vineyards, wild fynbos, and mountainous terrain — perfect for an elegant destination wedding on the mountain slopes & among the wine estates of the Hemel-en-Aarde Valley behind the seaside town of Hermanus.

Bona Dea is a ‘must-see’ venue!!  Your perfect year-round, all-weather venue!

This award-winning luxurious estate is ideally suited for a wedding ceremony and reception for you and your guests – Perfect for any indoor or outdoor ceremony or reception. The garden treed terrace, offering breathtaking views, is a tranquil spot for guests to enjoy a sundowner or served with an array of canapés.

With our outstanding level of service, Bona Dea Private Estate promises a memorable wedding at one of the best wedding destinations abroad. From the moment you enquire until you proceed down the aisle.
